# Aggregated Impact

**What it is:** Meta-analysis view across all experiments in a window. Answers 'did our experimentation program move our top-line metric?' rather than 'did experiment X move metric Y?'.

## Amplitude counterpart

- **Concept:** (No direct Amplitude equivalent)
- **Match quality:** none
- **Key differences:**
  - Related to holdouts but distinct — aggregates results across many experiments
  - Lets execs see net program impact, not just individual win/loss counts
